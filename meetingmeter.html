Connect Slack

Not connected

After saving a meeting, MeetingMeter can automatically post a cost summary to your Slack channel. Follow these 4 easy steps:

1
Open Slack in your browser
Go to api.slack.com/apps and sign in with your Slack account.
2
Create a new app
Click "Create New App" → choose "From scratch" → name it anything (e.g. "MeetingMeter") → select your workspace → click Create.
3
Enable Incoming Webhooks
On the left sidebar click "Incoming Webhooks" → toggle it ON → scroll down and click "Add New Webhook to Workspace" → pick a channel → click Allow.
4
Copy & paste the webhook URL below
You'll see a URL starting with https://hooks.slack.com/services/… — copy it and paste it in the box below.

Once connected, every saved meeting will offer to post a summary to your Slack channel automatically.

Connect Google Calendar

Not connected

Once connected, you can import upcoming events from your calendar to pre-fill the meeting title and duration with one click. Follow these 4 steps:

1
Open Google Calendar
Go to calendar.google.com in your browser and sign in.
2
Open Settings
Click the ⚙️ gear icon (top right) → click "Settings".
3
Find your calendar's secret link
On the left sidebar, click your calendar name under "Settings for my calendars" → scroll down to find "Secret address in iCal format" → click the copy button next to it.
4
Paste the link below
The link starts with https://calendar.google.com/calendar/ical/… — paste it below and click Test to confirm it works.

Your calendar link is private — it's stored only in your browser and never shared with anyone.

FAQ
How do I cancel my subscription?
Click "Manage or Cancel Subscription" above. You'll be taken to the Stripe portal where you can cancel in one click. You keep Pro access until the end of your billing period.
I switched devices — how do I restore Pro?
In the app, click "Upgrade to Pro" and use the "Restore with email" option at the bottom. Enter the email you paid with and your Pro access will be restored instantly.
Is my salary data stored anywhere?
No. All data lives only in your browser's local storage. Nothing is sent to our servers — not names, salaries, or meeting details.
Can I use MeetingMeter on multiple devices?
Yes. Use the email restore feature on any device to unlock Pro. Your meeting history stays per-browser since nothing is synced to a server.
What payment methods are accepted?
All major credit and debit cards via Stripe. Payments are secure and encrypted — we never see your card details.
