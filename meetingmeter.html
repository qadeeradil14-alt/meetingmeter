FAQ
How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel anytime through your Stripe customer portal. Your access will remain active until the end of your billing period.

Will my data be deleted if I cancel?

No, your meeting history and settings are stored locally on your device and will remain available even after cancellation.

Can I get an invoice?

Yes, invoices are available in your Stripe account. Visit your Stripe customer portal to download receipts and invoices.

How are my integrations secured?

All API keys and webhooks are stored locally in your browser. They are never sent to our servers or shared with third parties.

Do my integrations get deleted when I sign out?

No. Your integrations (Slack, Google Calendar, Claude, Zoom) persist locally on your device after logout. They'll be available when you log back in. If you're sharing a device, you can manually clear browser data to remove them.

What data does AgendaBurn collect?

AgendaBurn is 100% local. Meeting data, attendees, and settings stay on your device. No analytics, no tracking, no data collection.
